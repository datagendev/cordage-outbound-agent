## Notes
- Initial ICP: CDMOs, pharma/biotech manufacturers, API producers, 50-500 employees
- FDA letter types: CDER (drugs), CBER (biologics). Skip CFSAN (food) unless doc control cited.
- Priority contacts: VP Quality > Director Compliance > Head Regulatory Affairs > Quality Manager
- Disqualify: < 20 employees, > 1000 employees, cosmetics-only, supplements-only
- Timing: prioritize URGENT (< 15 days) and ACTIVE (15-90 days) over PREP
- Geography: global (FDA inspects international facilities)
