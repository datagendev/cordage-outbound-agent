# Cordage Context

## The Product

Cordage is an AI-powered compliance and document management platform that runs autonomous audits with full evidence trails. It supports major frameworks including ISO 9001, FDA regulations, IATF 16949, and CFR21 Part 11. The platform replaces manual audit preparation and document control with AI that continuously monitors compliance status and surfaces gaps before regulators do.

## Pain Segment (Research)

- **Segment**: CDMOs that just got an FDA warning letter citing document control failures
- **Pain**: Contract development and manufacturing organizations are receiving FDA warning letters because their document control systems cannot keep pace with regulatory requirements across multiple clients and product lines
- **Why Existential**: An FDA warning letter can halt production, trigger client contract cancellations, and permanently damage reputation. The next step after a warning letter is a consent decree or facility shutdown
- **Trigger**: Receipt of an FDA warning letter, 483 observation, or failed audit citing document control deficiencies
- **Segment Size**: CDMOs and pharma manufacturers with FDA-regulated operations
- **Composite Score**: 729
